57 /*----------------------------------------------------------------------------
58  * Periodicity management for INIMAS

60  * If INIMAS is called by NAVSTO :

62  * and is equal to the velocity one for the previous time step.

113  * dpdx, dpdy and dpdz gradient (1 -> n_cells_with_ghosts).
