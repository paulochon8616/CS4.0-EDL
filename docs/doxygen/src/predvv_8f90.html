Detailed Description

This subroutine perform the velocity prediction step of the Navier Stokes equations for incompressible or slightly compressible flows for the coupled velocity components solver.

  • at the first call, the predicted velocities are computed and also an estimator on the predicted velocity is computed.
  • at the second call, a global estimator on Navier Stokes is computed. This second call is done after the correction step (resopv).
